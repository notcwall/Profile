About Me

Hi. I'm a 23 year old student currently living in Austin, TX. I'm originally from the Sacramento area of California and have lived all over the country, including Maine, southeast Texas, and Wyoming. Outside of school, all of my time is divided between warehouse management and IT work for JJA Corp (a local Austin business), spending time with my wonderful girlfriend Patricia, and playing online video games with friends.

I'm currently enrolled in The Coding Boot Camp at UT Austin and aspire to quickly embark into the Web Development industry once I graduate. I have attended The University of Texas - Austin for several years now, part of which was spent was a Computer Science major, but ultimately decided to allocate all of my academic attention toward a focus in Web Development.
